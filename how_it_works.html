How It Works

1

Data Collection

The system aggregates historical sales data (Product, Date, Quantity) from the database.

2

Preprocessing

Data is cleaned and converted into a time-series format suitable for analysis.

3

AI Training

A Linear Regression model trains on the date vs. quantity relationship to find trends.

4

Prediction

The model delivers a demand forecast for the next month with a confidence score.

Model Explanation

We use Linear Regression (y = mx + c) where:

  • y is the Predicted Demand
  • x is the Time (Date)
  • m is the Trend (Slope)
This simple yet effective model identifies whether sales are generally increasing or decreasing over time.
